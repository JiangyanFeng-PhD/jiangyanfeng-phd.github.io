Biography

I am a fourth year Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I am also enrolled in Computational Science and Engineering Graduate Concentration Program.

My research focuses on developing and employing atomistic simulations, machine learning, and data science approaches for understanding complex biological processes (i.e. protein conformational dynamics).
